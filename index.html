Burgers.

We have partnered with Aubrey Allen to develop what we believe is THE best burger ever; it's the highest quality beef paired with a next level production method that ensures it has the most tender, fall apart mouth feel every single time.

Aubrey Allen beef is selected from native suckler beef breeds such as Angus, Hereford and Devon Reds and matured for a minimum of 21 days to enhance the texture and develop the flavour. When you combine this quality with our dreamy sauces and wondertricks it really is the stuff of burger fantasies.
